Stream 01

Cultural Architecture & Change Management

"Process is secondary; the people running it are primary."

We don't just hand over a new workflow — we embed the mindset required to sustain it. We help maritime organisations shift from traditional, siloed hierarchies to agile, cross-functional teams that can embrace and drive continuous improvement.

Organisational Health Audit
Identifying the cultural friction points that slow down ship design and production — before they become costly delays.
Agile for Maritime
Implementing Scrum and Kanban methodologies specifically tailored for the shipyard environment to increase transparency and delivery velocity.
Leadership Mentoring
Coaching executives on how to lead digital-first teams and manage the emotional journey of transformation — from resistance to ownership.
Stream 02

AI Agent Integration

"Liberating your engineers from the Data Tax."

Stop treating AI as a chatbot and start treating it as a specialised team member. We help you deploy AI Agents that act as co-pilots for your technical staff — allowing them to focus on creativity and strategic decisions while the agents handle the complexity.

Automated Design Agents
Deploying AI to handle repetitive naval architecture tasks — structural weight optimisation, compartment layouts, stability checks — reducing lead times significantly.
Knowledge-Base Agents
Creating proprietary AI systems that read your company's decades of legacy drawings and data, making tribal knowledge instantly accessible to every engineer.
Workflow Orchestration
Implementing AI agents that monitor project health, flagging bottlenecks in the design-to-production handoff before they cause costly delays.
Stream 03

Digital Shipbuilding Strategy

"Building the bridge between human intuition and digital precision."

We help shipyards move beyond basic CAD and PLM. We design a Digital Thread that ensures information flows seamlessly from the first sketch to the final weld — eliminating rework, reducing errors, and accelerating time to market.

Human-Centric Digital Twins
Developing digital twins that aren't just for monitoring machines, but for empowering the workforce with real-time, actionable data at every stage of the build.
Process De-Risking
Using simulation and AI to pre-build ships in a virtual environment — identifying human-factors issues and design clashes before they reach the shop floor.
Time-to-Market Acceleration
Auditing the full business lifecycle to identify where digital tools can shave weeks off the production schedule and improve margin.
Naval Architecture & Engineering

Where the Engineering Begins

Before transformation, there is craft. Our collective has spent decades in the drawing office and on the waterfront — delivering precise, bespoke engineering for owners and shipyards who demand the best.

01

Concept Design

From brief to vessel — with precision.

We develop vessel concepts from the initial owner's brief through to a comprehensive preliminary specification — hull form, general arrangement, stability parameters, propulsion selection, and key performance targets. Work that forms the backbone of every subsequent decision.

  • Hull form development & optimisation
  • General arrangement design
  • Stability & hydrostatics
  • Propulsion & power estimates
  • Preliminary weight & cost benchmarking
02

Owner's Engineer

Your interests, represented.

Shipbuilding is complex. Contracts are long. Shipyards have their own priorities. We act as the owner's independent technical representative throughout design and construction — making sure specifications are met, risks are flagged early, and your investment is protected at every stage.

  • Contract & specification review
  • Design approval & oversight
  • Shipyard progress monitoring
  • Sea trials & delivery support
  • Independent risk assessment

About R Marine Consultants

People, Culture, and Intelligent Augmentation

We are change agents who focus on the human side of maritime engineering — helping organisations build a culture of agility where talent is empowered by technology, not burdened by it.

Who We Are

A Specialist Collective, Delft Based

R Marine Consultants is a collective of naval architects and marine engineers united by deep technical expertise and a shared commitment to precision. We work with shipowners, shipyards, and investors across the full design, compliance, and transformation lifecycle.

We believe that a shipyard or design firm is not a collection of desks, drydocks, or computers — it is a living ecosystem of people and culture. Real improvement doesn't come from a manual on a shelf. It comes from shifting the mindsets of the people who execute the work.

Founded and led by a naval architect and marine engineer with over 20 years of hands-on experience across superyachts, offshore vessels, and commercial shipping, our collective brings together specialists with equally deep backgrounds — giving clients access to over 100 combined years of maritime expertise.

Based in Delft, we operate internationally — bringing the right expertise to any project, anywhere.
